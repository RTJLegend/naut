About Nautilus

We started with a pool and a mission. A decade later, we're shaping Oman's swimming future.

Founded in 2015

Nautilus Swimming Club was established in September 2015 to fill a gap in Oman's aquatic sports landscape. What started as a small coaching operation has grown into the country's leading swim program.

Our mission is straightforward: develop every individual's capacity to achieve excellence and life skills through competitive aquatic activities.

International competition.

We host significant long-course swim meets with international participation from Bahrain, Qatar, and the UAE. Our athletes regularly represent Oman at regional and international championships.

Swimming is not just a sport here — it's a discipline that builds character, resilience, and community.

What drives us

We believe every individual — regardless of age, background, or ability — deserves access to world-class aquatic training. Our programs are designed not just to build faster swimmers, but to instill discipline, confidence, and a lifelong love for the water.

That's the culture we've been building for nearly a decade, and it's why families across Oman trust Nautilus with their children's development.
